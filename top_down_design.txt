1 How to become a Turing student
  1.1 Discover your interest in coding
    1.1.1 Do research on coding
      1.1.1.1 Locate computer
      1.1.1.2 Walk to computer
        1.1.1.2.1 Pick up left leg
        1.1.1.2.2 Take step
        1.1.1.2.3 Pick up right leg
        1.1.1.2.4 Take step
        1.1.1.2.5 Repeat until destination has been reached
      1.1.1.3 Sit at computer
        1.1.1.3.1 Face behind towards chair
        1.1.1.3.2 Bend Knees
        1.1.1.3.3 Place behind in chair
      1.1.1.4 Turn on computer
        1.1.1.4.1 Locate power button
        1.1.1.4.2 Press it
      1.1.1.5 Enter password
        1.1.1.5.1 Locate password input field
        1.1.1.5.2 Enter password
        1.1.1.5.3 Press enter
      1.1.1.6 Grab mouse and keyboard
        1.1.1.6.1 Place dominant hand on mouse
        1.1.1.6.2 Place non dominant hand on keyboard
      1.1.1.7 Open google chrome
        1.1.1.7.1 Locate google chrome on desktop
        1.1.1.7.2 Double Click to open
      1.1.1.8 Type in google.com into URL
        1.1.1.8.1 Press Command+L to highlight URL
        1.1.1.8.2 Type google.com
        1.1.1.8.3 Press enter
      1.1.1.9 Select search field
        1.1.1.9.1 Locate search field
        1.1.1.9.2 Click on it
      1.1.1.10 Type in web development
        1.1.10.1 While search field is selected type in web development
      1.1.1.11 Select desired URL in google
        1.1.1.11.1 Locate desired article
        1.1.1.11.2 Click it
      1.1.1.12 Read about coding
  1.2 Apply
    1.2.1 Locate website for Turing
      1.2.1.1 Command+L
      1.2.1.2 Type in turing.io into URL
    1.2.2 Choose between front-end or back-end program
      1.2.2.1 Choose Front-end
        1.2.2.1.1 Locate front end apply button
        1.2.2.1.2 Click it
    1.2.3 Go through all of the questions asked in application
      1.2.3.1 Submit resume
        1.2.3.1.1 Locate resume
        1.2.3.1.2 upload it
      1.2.3.2 Do logic test
    1.2.5 Submit application
1.3 Interview
    1.3.1 Schedule Interview
      1.3.1.1 Select desired time for interview via chrome
      1.3.1.2 Confirm interview date and time
    1.3.2 Go to Turing
      1.3.2.1 Drive to address
        1.3.2.1.1 Locate Car
        1.3.2.1.2 Walk to Car
        1.3.2.1.3 Get in Car
        1.3.2.1.4 Turn on Car
        1.3.2.1.5 commence drive
      1.3.2.2 Find Parking
        1.3.2.2.1 Locate suitable parking spot
      1.3.2.3 Park Car
        1.3.2.3.1 slowly drive car into desired parking area
      1.3.2.4 Walk to Turing
        1.3.2.4.1 Turn off Car
        1.3.2.4.2 Get out of Car
        1.3.2.4.3 Lock Car
        1.3.2.4.4 Commence walk
      1.3.2.5 conduct interview with teacher
        1.3.2.4.1 Take tour
        1.3.2.5.2 Answer questions
        1.3.2.5.3 do logic games
